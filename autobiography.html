📖 My Autobiography

From Bandwar to the World — My Life Story

🌅 Born in Bandwar, Begusarai — 13 March 2008

I was born on 13 March 2008 in Bandwar, Begusarai, Bihar. My father told me that when I was born, my mother said, "Name him Ravi, because he will bring light to this house." Since then, I have been the light of my family.

🏏 Childhood — Cricket, School & Papa's Cycle

My childhood was simple and beautiful. Waking up at 6 AM, cycling to school with friends — this was my daily routine. I loved cricket. Once I played for the school team but got out early. That day my father said, "Getting out is not a big deal, keep playing." I still remember that line whenever I fail in coding.

📚 School Days — Where Coding Started

My favorite subject in school was Computer Science. I also loved Maths because it had logic — and coding is all about logic. In 8th grade, I saw HTML code for the first time in the computer lab. I thought, "This is magic!" My teacher said this is called coding. I wanted to build my first webpage that very day. In 10th grade, I built my first webpage — just a "Hello World." But when it appeared on screen, I felt pure joy. That day I decided — "This is my career."

👨‍👩‍👧 Family — My Biggest Strength

My father is my role model. He taught me, "Keep working hard, results will follow." My mother taught me, "Help others, it always comes back." My younger sister calls me "bhaiya" with so much love. For me, family is the people who motivate me. Whenever I feel frustrated with coding, my mother says, "Learn slowly, no one asks how much time you took."

💻 Coding Journey — From 2023 to Today

I started coding in 2023 with HTML and CSS. In 2024, I learned JavaScript and Python. In 2025, I explored React, Node.js, and backend. In 2024, I built my first portfolio website. It was simple, but it gave me the confidence that I can do something. In 2026, I launched this professional website that you are seeing now.

🎯 My Dream — Full-Stack Developer

I want to become a Full-Stack Developer — someone who builds real solutions. My next goals are: strengthen backend & databases, build real-world projects, and one day launch my own startup.

❤️ Friends, Love & Life

My friends — Sudhanshu, Rohit, Sitanashu — have never let me feel alone. I believe life is boring without friends. No love life yet, but I believe "everything happens at the right time."

🌍 My Message For Everyone

If you come from a small village, don't think you can't achieve anything. I started from zero — with just one HTML tag. Today, I am writing my own website that can be seen from anywhere in the world. No struggle is permanent. Consistency and patience make everything possible.

📌 18 Years Journey — Summary

  • 0–5: Born in Bandwar, happiness with family
  • 6–10: School, friends, cricket, cycle
  • 11–14: First coding in computer lab
  • 15–16: Learned HTML/CSS, first webpage
  • 17: Learned JavaScript & Python
  • 18: Portfolio launch, professional journey begins

🌅 Janam — Bandwar, Begusarai — 13 March 2008

Mera janam 13 March 2008 ko Bandwar, Begusarai, Bihar mein hua. Papa ne bataya ki jab main paida hua, toh mummy ne kaha, "Iska naam Ravi rakho, kyunki yeh ghar mein roshni layega." Tab se main apni family ki roshni ban gaya.

🏏 Bachpan — Cricket, School aur Papa Ki Cycle

Mera bachpan bahut simple aur beautiful tha. Subah 6 baje uthna, cycle nikalna, aur doston ke saath school jaana — yeh mera daily routine tha. Mujhe cricket bahut pasand thi. Ek baar maine school team ke liye match khela tha, lekin main jaldi out ho gaya. Us din papa ne mujhe kaha, "Out hona koi badi baat nahi, khelte rehna." Aaj bhi main jab coding mein fail hota hoon, toh papa ki woh baat yaad aati hai.

📚 School Days — Jahaan Coding Ki Shuruaat Hui

School mein mera favourite subject Computer Science tha. Mujhe Maths bhi pasand thi, kyunki usme logic tha — aur coding bhi logic hi hai. 8th class mein maine pehli baar computer lab mein HTML code dekha. Mujhe laga — "Yeh toh magic hai!" Teacher ne bataya ki isko coding kehte hain. Main usi din apna pehla webpage banana chahta tha. 10th class mein maine apna pehla webpage banaya — bas ek "Hello World" tha. Lekin jab screen par aaya, toh mujhe bahut khushi mili. Tab se maine soch liya — "Yahi mera career hai."

👨‍👩‍👧 Family — Meri Sabse Badi Strength

Papa mere role model hain. Unhone mujhe sikhaya — "Mehnat karte raho, result apne aap aayega." Mummy ne mujhe sikhaya — "Dusron ki help karo, uska fal milta hai." Meri chhoti behen mujhe "bhaiya" kehkar bahut pyaar karti hai. Mere liye family woh log hain jo mujhe motivate karte hain. Jab main coding mein frustrate hota hoon, toh mummy kehti hain — "Dheere dheere seekho, koi nahi poochhta ki tumne kitna time liya."

💻 Coding Journey — 2023 Se Lekar Aaj Tak

Maine 2023 mein coding seekhna shuru kiya — HTML aur CSS. 2024 mein JavaScript aur Python seekha. 2025 mein React, Node.js, aur backend explore kiya. 2024 mein maine apna pehla portfolio website banaya. Woh simple tha, lekin usne mujhe confidence diya ki main kuch kar sakta hoon. 2026 mein maine yeh professional website launch ki — jo aap dekh rahe hain.

🎯 Mera Sapna — Full-Stack Developer

Main Full-Stack Developer banna chahta hoon — jo real problems solve kare. Mere next goals hain: backend aur databases strong karna, real-world projects banana, aur ek din apna startup launch karna.

❤️ Friends, Love aur Life

Mere dost hain — Sudhanshu, Rohit, Sitanashu. Unhone mujhe kabhi akela feel nahi karne diya. Main maanta hoon ki doston ke bina life boring hai. Abhi tak love life nahi hai, lekin main believe karta hoon — "Sab kuch apne time pe hota hai."

🌍 Mera Message — Sabhi Ke Liye

Agar aap bhi kisi chhote gaon se hain, toh yeh mat sochiye ki aap kuch nahi kar sakte. Maine bhi zero se shuru kiya tha — ek HTML tag se. Aaj main apni website likh raha hoon, jo duniya ke kisi bhi kone se dekhi ja sakti hai. Koi bhi struggle permanent nahi hai. Consistency aur patience se sab kuch possible hai.
