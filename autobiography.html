🌅 Chapter 1: The Beginning (2008–2012)

📸 Upload Chapter Photo

I was born on 13 March 2008 in Begusarai, Bihar. My father told me that when I was born, my mother said, "Name him Ravi, because he will bring light to this house." Since then, I have been the light of my family.

My childhood memories are as many as any child from a small village could have. Cycling in the courtyard, sitting on the roof in the evening, and eating food cooked by my mother — all of these are still alive in my mind.

One day, I stole my sister's cycle and joined a race — and fell straight into a gutter! My father picked me up and said, "Beta, pehle balance seekh, phir speed." Today, I still love cycling — but I avoid gutters.
