MY STORY

Who I Am, Where I Come From

This is not just a bio. This is my journey.

I was born and raised in Bandwar, Begusarai, Bihar — a small town where people don't usually think about coding. But I was always curious about how things work. The first time I opened a computer, I was fascinated. That curiosity turned into passion.

In 2023, I wrote my first line of HTML — `

Hello World

`. It wasn't much, but it opened a door. Since then, I've been obsessed with learning everything I can about web development. I've built projects, made mistakes, stayed up late, and loved every moment of it.

I'm still a student. I'm still learning. But I know one thing for sure — I want to build things that matter. I want to create websites that solve problems and make people's lives better. And I'm not stopping until I get there.

This website is a reflection of who I am — my work, my thoughts, my world. Thanks for stopping by. I hope you find something that inspires you.
