My Story

From Begusarai to building for the web

I'm Ravi Raj, born and raised in Begusarai, Bihar. I started coding in 2023 with a single HTML tag. That simple <h1> changed everything.

Today, I build websites that work for real people. I believe in clean code, responsive design, and continuous learning.

I'm currently in 12th grade, balancing studies with my passion for web development. This website is my digital diary — where I share my journey, my projects, and my thoughts.
